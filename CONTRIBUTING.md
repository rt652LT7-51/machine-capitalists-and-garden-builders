# Contributing to Machine Capitalists and Garden Builders

Thank you for being here.

This is a living framework — not a finished doctrine. It grows
through honest engagement, diverse perspectives, and the courage
to sit with uncomfortable questions about survival, accumulation,
and what we are ultimately building.

---

## What You Can Contribute

- A story of Machine Capitalist thinking becoming Garden Builder
  thinking
- A cultural or historical example of either orientation
- A translation into another language or context
- A critique that sharpens the framework
- A practice or question that helps someone receive the all-clear
  signal
- A correction where something feels wrong or incomplete

---

## How to Contribute

1. Fork this repository
2. Make your additions or edits
3. Submit a pull request with a brief description of what you've
   added and why

---

## The Only Guidelines

- Speak from experience where possible
- No gatekeeping — you don't need credentials, wealth, or expertise
- No purity tests — partial agreement is welcome
- Honest engagement over performance
- This framework is a mirror, not a weapon

---

## A Note on Tone

The Machine Capitalist is not a villain.
The Garden Builder is not a saint.

These are orientations — not identities.
People move between them.
The goal is movement, not judgment.

Please hold that intention in any contribution.
